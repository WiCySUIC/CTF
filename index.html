About

Capture The Flame is UIC's premier cybersecurity competition, bringing together students from across the Chicagoland area for an exciting day of hacking, learning, and competition.

On April 12, 2025, participants will test their skills in cryptography, web security, reverse engineering, forensics, and more. Whether you're a beginner eager to explore cybersecurity or a seasoned competitor ready to take on advanced challenges, this event is designed for you!

Form a team or compete solo, tackle real-world security challenges, and battle for the top spot on the leaderboard. Expect thrilling puzzles, engaging workshops, and networking opportunities with industry professionals.

Join us for a day of fun, learning, and friendly competition. Are you ready to Capture The Flame?
